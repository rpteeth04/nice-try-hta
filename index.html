Why did I create this interactive HTA case study?

By combining this interactive case study with my ongoing preparation for advanced credentials in Health Economics, I wanted to explore macro-level health system value beyond individual clinical outcomes. This project served as a playground for me to practice translating complex medical evidence into structured, population-level resource optimization models while experimenting with how Generative AI can accelerate visual tool creation. This is very much an evolving learning journey for me, and I warmly welcome any feedback, insights, or advice from experienced professionals in the field!

— Dr. Ratnapriya Thakur, a dentist exploring healthcare beyond clinics.
