Clinical Efficacy vs. Macroeconomic System Access

🌟 Project Overview

This interactive portfolio piece represents a practical exploration of Health Technology Assessment (HTA) concepts, bridging the gap between frontline clinical medicine and macro-level health system resource optimization.

Developed by a clinician transitioning into the strategic market access space, this sandbox models the real-world friction that public healthcare payers (like NICE in the UK) face when managing high-prevalence chronic diseases. It translates clinical trial data from the pivotal SURMOUNT-1 study (focusing on a dual GIP/GLP-1 receptor agonist) into scalable, population-level budget impact simulations.

📊 Health Economic Methodology

The budget impact sandbox models the macroeconomic strain of broad medical guidelines using three distinct coverage pathways:

Unrestricted Pathway ($E = 1.0$): Standard marketing authorization parameters (BMI $\ge 30\text{ kg/m}^2$ + 1 comorbidity) capturing approximately $3.4\text{M}$ eligible individuals in the UK.

NICE Restricted Pathway ($E = 0.35$): Guarded access (severe obesity with BMI $\ge 35\text{ kg/m}^2$ + 1 comorbidity), optimizing cost-utility to within the standard $\pounds 20,000$ to $\pounds 30,000$ per QALY margin.

NHSE Year 1 Phased Priority ($E = 0.05$): Strategic sequential rollout targeting high-risk individuals (BMI $\ge 40\text{ kg/m}^2$ + multiple comorbidities) to protect upfront primary care infrastructure.

Mathematical Algorithm

The Gross Annual System Budget Impact ($I_{\text{gross}}$) is calculated in real-time as:

$$I_{\text{gross}} = P_{\text{base}} \times E \times C_{\text{annual}}$$

Where:

$P_{\text{base}}$ = Total Target Population Base ($1.0\text{M}$ to $5.0\text{M}$ patients)

$E$ = Eligibility Index/Preset Modifier ($0.05$, $0.35$, or $1.0$)

$C_{\text{annual}}$ = Annual Acquisition Drug Cost ($\pounds 500$ to $\pounds 2,500$ per patient)

The Resource Savings/Opportunity Cost Avoided ($S_{\text{avoided}}$) is calculated relative to the unrestricted market baseline:

$$S_{\text{avoided}} = (P_{\text{base}} \times C_{\text{annual}}) - I_{\text{gross}}$$
